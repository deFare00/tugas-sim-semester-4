About Us

"Embark on a Culinary Journey through Time: Unveiling the Tale of our Exquisite Japanese Restaurant in Depok. Rooted in the Year 1997, a Fateful Era that Marked the Birth of Our Gastronomic Haven. Conceived by Five Brilliant Young Souls, Each a Product of Gunadarma University's Esteemed Institution of Knowledge and Innovation. United by a Passion for Authentic Japanese Cuisine, they Poured their Hearts and Minds into Crafting an Experience that Transcends the Palate. Decades of Dedication and Expertise have Shaped our Culinary Masterpieces, Infusing the Finest Ingredients with Traditional Techniques and Contemporary Flair. With Every Sip and Savory Bite, Our Time-Honored Legacy Echoes, Welcoming You to Indulge in the Flavors and Warmth that Define our Enduring Restaurant."
